Product 01

Crush Dust

Crush dust is a fine quarry by-product commonly used for compaction, bedding, blinding layers, and surface preparation where a tighter fill material is needed.

  • Used under paving, walkways, blocks, and smaller foundation work.
  • Helps create a compacted base with a smoother finished level.
  • Suitable for contractors, yard collection, and bulk supply requests.

Product 02

13mm Crush Stone

13mm crush stone is a versatile aggregate for concrete production, general building works, and construction jobs that need a reliable medium-size stone.

  • Ideal for concrete mixes, floor slabs, and structural casting work.
  • Consistent stone size helps with cleaner batching and placement.
  • Available for site delivery, retail customers, and larger project orders.

Product 03

19mm Crush Stone

19mm crush stone is a larger aggregate used where stronger support, better drainage, and heavier-duty construction performance are required.

  • Suitable for drainage layers, road base support, and larger concrete work.
  • Provides a stronger stone option for demanding ground conditions.
  • Supplied for contractors, municipalities, and infrastructure jobs.

Product 04

Gabion Stone

Gabion stone consists of larger quarry rock selected for retaining structures, erosion protection, and civil works where durability and stone mass are important.

  • Used in gabion baskets, retaining walls, and erosion-control installations.
  • Well suited to slope protection, river work, and support structures.
  • Available for landscape, civil, and heavy outdoor construction supply.
