Jabulani Quarries

Quarry Information

Jabulani Crush & Quarry is an open cast mine nestled in the mountains of Tsolo, Eastern Cape, South Africa. We produce premium crushed stone, aggregate and sand for the construction industry. Established as part of the Jabulani Group in 2002, our quarry holds an estimated large-scale stone resource at depths of over 30 m and delivers consistently high-quality material, from foundation aggregate to road-base gravel, to contractors, municipalities and retailers across the Transkei.

Our aggregate is tested to meet SANS 1083 standards, ensuring every load that leaves our gates contributes to structures built to last a lifetime.
